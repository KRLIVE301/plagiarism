punishment for the academic plagiarism

Punishment for academic plagiarism can occur at two the teacher students relationship and at the student institution relationship. In general punishment established by teacher tend to be softer, without, however, this tendancy being rule. In general punishment by teacher are verbal or written warning, or by charge of marks or the final evolution of the student's course or activity. Institutional sanctions may include replements, education for learning academic integrity, social works, suspension, explusion, rewotation of the title(s) granted(s) and Even legal penalties, in accordance with current legislation (maurer et al., 2006).
