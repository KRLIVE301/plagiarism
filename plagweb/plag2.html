Plagiarism

different types of plagiarism

Direct Plagiarism This type of Plagiarism occurs when the user copies the work of
another person, word to word, without acknowledging the original
author or quoting the text.
self plagiarism It is the type of plagiarism where the author uses his own original
text in more than one contexts without citing that it was used earlier
also. The reader is made to believe that the text is original in every
context that it is used. Even though this act is not illegal. It is
definitely not ethical.
Mosaic plagiarism This type of plagiarism happens when the same sentence structure
is used with synonyms for the appropriate words of the sentence.
Accidental plagiarism It is when the user does not really understand how to properly
paraphrase, quote, and cite the research.
